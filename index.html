What We Do

End-to-End Liquor & Hospitality Solutions

From new license applications to renewals and compliance — we offer comprehensive support across all aspects of liquor licensing and hospitality regulation in South Africa.

Liquor License Application

New liquor license applications for restaurants, bars, retail liquor stores, nightclubs, and taverns — fully managed from start to approval.

Transfers & Seller-Ties

Seamless transfer of liquor licenses between entities, new locations, or upon sale of a business. Full seller-tie arrangement management included.

Renewals & Compliance

Timely annual license renewals and ongoing compliance management to keep your business fully legal, operational, and inspection-ready.

Compliance Audits

Thorough on-site compliance audits to identify and remediate risks before they become costly violations or license suspensions.

Company Registration & CSD

Business entity registration and Central Supplier Database (CSD) enrollment — ensuring your business is correctly structured from day one.

Zoning & Directions Agreement

Municipal zoning compliance verification and directional agreement processing to satisfy local authority requirements for your premises.

Transfer to Directors

Professional management of liquor license transfers upon change of company directorship, ownership restructuring, or share transfers.

Utility & Occupancy Certificates

Assistance with Certificates of Occupancy, utility account setup, and municipal clearance certificates required as part of licensing.

Business Licensing

General business licensing, municipal trading permits, and health certificates for hospitality and retail establishments across Gauteng.

Legal Document Drafting

Lease agreements, shareholder agreements, notices of public hearings, and all licensing-related legal documentation professionally drafted.

Hospitality Consulting

End-to-end strategic consulting for new and existing hospitality ventures — from concept planning to full regulatory compliance.

Temporary License Applications

Fast-tracked temporary and special event liquor license applications for functions, festivals, pop-up venues, and one-off occasions.
